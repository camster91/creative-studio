Pricing FAQ

What does "bring your own key" mean?

Paste a free Google AI Studio key in the editor sidebar. We never see or store your key on the server. Every generation goes directly from your browser to Google — and Google bills you, not us.

What if I use the shared key?

The server uses a shared Gemini key to generate images. Your prompts and product photos still go directly to Google's API (we don't log them). You share the daily cost cap with other users, default $5/day.

Are there monthly plans?

Not yet. We're building them — see the roadmap. For now, the per-image model is the lowest-friction way to scale.

Why does Ultra cost 12x more than Fast?

It's the same product photo in 2K with the Pro model. The Pro model produces noticeably more detail on labels, packaging, and reflections. For most CPG and DTC use, Balanced is the sweet spot.

Can I get a refund if I don't like the image?

There's nothing to refund — you pay Google directly (or the shared quota gets a bit lower). Re-generate as many times as you want, only successful generations are billed by Google.
