Public roadmap

No private Trello. Here's what we're building, in priority order, with rough dates.

Now — Q2 2026

  • Per-user image library

    Every generation saves to your account, filterable by date / scene / aspect ratio. Re-download, re-prompt, re-export. shipping this week

  • Accounts (email + password, no 3rd-party)

    Sign up with email. Per-user API key storage. No Google, no GitHub, no Clerk. shipping this week

  • Public share links

    Every image gets a permanent URL. Send to your team, your client, your agency. No login needed to view. shipping this week

  • Marketing site + comparison pages

    Real /pricing, /features, /customers, /changelog, /compare/photogen-vs-X. The site you're on now. shipping this week

Next — Q3 2026

  • On-model photography

    Generate images of your product being held by a model, on a person, in lifestyle scenes with humans. Like Flair's flagship feature. This is the single biggest request from agencies.

  • Direct Shopify publish

    One-click push to a Shopify store as a new product image. OAuth + Shopify Admin API integration.

  • Team workspaces

    Multiple seats under one organization. Shared brand library (logos, color tokens, scene presets). Admin console for usage and billing.

  • Webhook integrations

    Subscribe to image.generated and image.failed events. Pipe outputs to S3, Drive, Dropbox, or your own CDN.

Later — Q4 2026 and beyond

  • Brand-preset library

    Save your color tokens, scene presets, and product prompts as a brand kit. Apply to every new product with one click.

  • Video generation

    Static images are just the start. Once the static workflow is locked in, we move to short product videos (5–10s) for paid social.

  • Self-hosted Pro tier

    The current single-container deploy is fine for one team. A multi-tenant self-hosted version with org management, RBAC, and usage quotas for agencies who want to host Photogen for their clients.

Want something on the roadmap?
