Built by Ashbi Design

A small Toronto agency. We've been making ecommerce sites and brand assets for CPG and DTC brands since 2018. Creative Studio started as an internal tool to speed up our own product mockups.

Why this exists

Every CPG brand we've worked with has the same problem: they need 20–50 product images per SKU, across 6 aspect ratios, for 4–8 channels. Studio shoots cost $500–$2000 per SKU. Stock photos look like stock photos. Generic AI tools hallucinate label text and ruin the brand.

So we built a tool that uses your actual product photo and composites it into AI-generated scenes. The product is real. The scene is generated. The cost is cents, not hundreds of dollars.

What we believe

  • Your product is sacred. Never generate a fake version of a real product.
  • BYOK is the right default. If you're going to spend money on AI, you should spend it directly with the model provider, not have us skim a margin.
  • Self-hosting is a feature. Your product photos should never leave infrastructure you control.
  • Pay for what you use. No subscriptions, no minimums, no per-seat licensing.

Who uses it

Solo founders launching their first SKU. Mid-size CPG teams running 5–10 launches a quarter. Agencies producing assets for multiple client brands. Anyone who's ever paid $1,500 for a single product shoot and thought "there has to be a better way."
