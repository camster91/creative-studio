Last updated June 11, 2026

1. What this is

Creative Studio (a.k.a. Photogen) is a self-hosted AI product photography tool operated by Ashbi Design ("we", "us"). When you use photogen.ashbi.ca, you're using our hosted instance. The full source is open and you can also self-host.

2. Your data

Your product photos, prompts, and generated images stay on our infrastructure. We do not train any model on your inputs. We do not share your inputs or outputs with any third party except Google (when we call their API on your behalf in the shared-key flow).

3. Your API key

If you paste your own Gemini API key, it is stored in your browser's localStorage only. It is never sent to our server. Every generation with your key goes directly from your browser to Google's API.

4. Cost

If you use the shared key, you share a daily cost cap with other users. We may adjust the cap at any time. If you use your own key, you pay Google directly and we never see the cost.

  • Is intended to defraud or scam

6. Refunds

There are no charges on our end — you pay Google directly for API usage. We don't have a billing relationship with you for the hosted instance during the public beta. If we add paid plans later, refunds will be at our discretion.

7. Termination

We may shut down or restrict the hosted instance at any time. We will give 30 days notice on the changelog. After shutdown, all your data is deleted. You should download your library before then.
